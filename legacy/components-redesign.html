FAQ

Have Questions?

Find answers to common questions about our printing services, turnaround times, and ordering process.

What file formats do you accept?

We accept PDF, AI, EPS, PSD, and high-resolution JPG or PNG files. Vector files with outlined fonts work best for crisp results.

What is your turnaround time?

Standard turnaround is 3-5 business days. Rush options available for an additional fee. Large format items may require extra time.

Do you offer design services?

Yes! Our in-house design team offers free consultations and can create or modify artwork for your project at competitive rates.

Do you provide installation?

Yes, we offer professional installation for vehicle wraps, wall graphics, window films, and large signage with certified installers.

Do you ship nationwide?

Absolutely! We serve Northeast Ohio locally and ship print orders and promotional products nationwide.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, checks, and offer net-30 terms for established business accounts.
