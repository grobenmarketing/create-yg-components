Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services, processes, and what to expect.

How long does a vehicle wrap last?

With proper care, a high-quality vehicle wrap can last 5-7 years. We use premium 3M and Avery materials that are designed for durability and UV protection.

What file formats do you accept?

We accept AI, EPS, PDF, PSD, and high-resolution JPEG/PNG files. For best results, please provide vector files with outlined fonts.

Do you offer design services?

Yes! Our in-house creative team can help with everything from logo design to complete brand identity packages.

What is your turnaround time?

Turnaround varies by project. Simple print jobs may be ready in 2-3 business days, while larger projects like vehicle wraps typically take 1-2 weeks. We always provide a timeline estimate with your quote.

Do you offer installation services?

Yes, we provide professional installation for vehicle wraps, wall graphics, window graphics, and signage. Our certified installers ensure a flawless finish.

Can I get a quote online?

Absolutely! Use our online quote form or give us a call at 800-370-9694 for a free estimate.
